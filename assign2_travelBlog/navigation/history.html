HISTORY OF AUSTRALIA

Rich and Ancient Heritage

Australia's history dates back over 65,000 years, with the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples being the original inhabitants. Their rich cultures, traditions, and deep connection to the land remain central to Australia's identity.

In 1770, Captain James Cook claimed the eastern coast for Britain, leading to European settlement in 1788. Since then, Australia has transformed into a diverse and modern nation.
